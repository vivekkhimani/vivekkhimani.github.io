About

Vivek is an undergraduate sophomore at Drexel University pursuing a BS in Computer Science with a prospected minors in Mathematics and Data Science. Vivek is extremely passionate about his interests in mathematics, data science, machine learning, and blockchains. As a result, he plans to pursue concentrations like - Artificial Intelligence, Computer Vision, Computer Networks and Security, Computing Systems (OS & Concurrent Programming), and Data Structures & Algorithms. He is also planning to pursue minors in Mathematics & Data Science.

In addition to the solid academic record, Vivek has also been actively working as a researcher, campus organizations leader, teaching assistant, and part-time intern at on-campus startups. All the information about his projects, skills, experiences, and more can be found on his personal website (see below) and LinkedIn profile.

Vivek recently attended an engineering exchange program at Hong Kong University of Science and Technology in Fall 2019. During his time there, he got involved in professional machine learning research with the professor(s) and PhD student(s). Since then, he has continued his research and is currently working on solving the data pollution threats associated with the federated machine learning systems. At Drexel, he is currently serving as a teaching assistant for the Advanced Programming Techniques (CS 265) class, and a vice-president for Data Structures & Algorithms Club. He is also a member of Drexel Data Science Group and Drexel Google Developers Student Club.
